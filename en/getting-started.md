# Getting Started with Numeroo

Numeroo is a personal finance app that tracks your expenses and income — with **AI-powered receipt scanning** that reads and imports your receipts automatically.

## What you can do

- 📸 Scan receipts with AI and import items instantly
- 💸 Track expenses and income manually
- 📊 Analyze spending patterns with detailed statistics
- 💰 Set budgets per category and monitor them in real time
- 🏦 Manage multiple assets (cash, bank accounts, wallets)
- 🎯 Set a savings goal and track your progress
- 📅 Define your own monthly cycle start date

## How to navigate

- 🏠 **Home** — your financial overview at a glance
- 📊 **Statistics** — spending pace, trends and charts
- ⇄ **Center button** — add a transaction or scan a receipt
- 📂 **Budget** — budgets per category
- ⚙️ **Settings** — account, assets, categories and preferences
