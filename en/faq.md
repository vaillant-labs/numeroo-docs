## Can I send cat photos to Numeroo?

Unfortunately our AI is trained on receipts, not cats 🐱. If you try, it will probably try to find a subtotal on your cat's forehead.

<br/>

## The AI matched my items to the wrong categories

The AI does its best to match receipt items to your existing categories, but it can make mistakes — especially with items that have ambiguous names. Always review the extracted items before confirming. You can tap any item to change its category manually.

<br/>

## I created a transaction but it doesn't show on the home screen

The home screen filters transactions by **type** (Expense or Income) and **date range**. Check the toggles at the top — if you're viewing Expenses but the transaction was created as Income, or the date range doesn't include the transaction date, it won't appear. Adjust the filters to find it.

<br/>

## I can't find a transaction

Tap **View all** next to Latest Transactions on the home screen to see the full list. Make sure the **date range** and **type** selectors include the period and type of the transaction you're looking for. If you registered it on a past date, select a date range that covers it.

<br/>

## How do I change the date of a transaction?

Tap the transaction to open its details, then tap **Edit** in the top right. Tap the green bar at the bottom to open the date picker and select the correct date. Tap **Save** to confirm.

<br/>

## Why are my spending estimates so high?

The Spending Pace projects your spending to the end of the cycle based on your current rhythm. Large expenses at the start of the cycle — like rent or utility bills — will temporarily push estimates very high. As the cycle progresses and spending normalises, the estimates will adjust and come down.

<br/>

## Why do my statistics show everything in red and no income?

This usually means no income has been recorded for the current cycle. Either you haven't added an income transaction yet, or the income was added on a date that falls outside the current cycle range. Check your cycle start date under Settings → Preferences and make sure your income transaction date falls within the current cycle.
